THE IMPORTANCE OF BIOMES

Case Study: Sparrows in Ahmedabad

What is a Biome?

  • A biome is a region with a distinct climate and life forms
  • Includes natural (forests, deserts) and urban (cities) biomes
  • Biomes support ecosystems and biodiversity

üè†
Ahmedabad's Urban Biome & Sparrows

üê¶

Sparrows once thrived in old Ahmedabad due to:

  • Open structures
  • Grain availability
  • Small gardens

Modern changes:

  • Sealed buildings
  • Pesticides
  • Fewer insects

üí°
Why It Matters

  • Sparrows are indicators of a healthy biome
  • Their decline shows urban habitat loss
  • Conservation efforts: nest boxes, awareness, traditional designs

üéØ
Conclusion

Healthy biomes keep nature balanced
Even cities can support biodiversity
Small changes can bring big impact
